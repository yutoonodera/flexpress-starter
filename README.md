## Content Feeds

FlexPress Starter allows you to display filtered posts anywhere in your website.

You can control what content appears on each page based on category, store, location, or other conditions.

### Examples

- Show only news posts on the homepage  
- Display store-specific posts on store pages  
- Display area-based content on location pages  
- Show category-specific blogs on service pages  

### Capabilities

- Filter posts by category, tag, or custom taxonomy  
- Display content by store or location  
- Embed post lists into any page  
- Customize layout (list, card, etc.)
